# Key Management Centre (KMC)

**Source:** [[subset26-1]] | **Date:** 2026-05-12 | **Type:** entity

The Key Management Centre (KMC) manages cryptographic keys used to secure EURORADIO communications between ERTMS/ETCS entities (on-board equipment, RBCs and RIUs). [subset26-1 ¶123]
