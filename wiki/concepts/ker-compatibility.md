# KER Compatibility

**Source:** [[subset40]] | **Date:** 2026-05-07 | **Type:** concept

**KER compatibility** refers to additional requirements for equipment that supports KER-compatible transmission systems alongside ERTMS/ETCS. These requirements are not required for ERTMS/ETCS interoperability itself [¶193](http://localhost:8765/go.html#subset40-193).

## Requirements

Must respect:
- SUBSET-100 §§4, 6 and Annexes [¶194](http://localhost:8765/go.html#subset40-194).
- SUBSET-101 §4.1.5 [¶194](http://localhost:8765/go.html#subset40-194).
- Subsections 5.1.1.2 and 5.1.1.3 are intentionally deleted [¶195](http://localhost:8765/go.html#subset40-195).
