# End Of Loop Marker (EOLM)

**Source:** [[subset26]] | **Date:** 2025-07-17 | **Type:** entity

The End Of Loop Marker (EOLM) is a balise group that marks the beginning or end of a Euroloop and provides identity and preparation information for loop reception. [subset26 ¶1102][subset26 ¶1103]

## Information Provided

The EOLM transmits [subset26 ¶1111][subset26 ¶1112][subset26 ¶1113][subset26 ¶1114][subset26 ¶1115][subset26 ¶1116]:

- **Loop identity** — identifies the loop ahead
- **Spread spectrum key** — for loop communication decoding
- **Distance to loop** — distance from EOLM to the loop start
- **Loop length** — physical length of the loop
- **Orientation indicator** — direction of the loop relative to the EOLM
