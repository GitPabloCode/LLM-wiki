# Key Management Centre (KMC)

**Source:** [[subset26]] | **Date:** 2025-07-17 | **Type:** entity

The Key Management Centre (KMC) manages cryptographic keys used to secure EURORADIO communications between ERTMS/ETCS entities (on-board equipment, RBCs, RIUs). [subset26 ¶123]

## Related Infrastructure

- **Public Key Infrastructure (PKI)**: Manages and distributes digital certificates to allow secure online distribution of cryptographic keys between KMCs and ERTMS/ETCS entities. [subset26 ¶125]

## Purpose

The KMC ensures the security of Euroradio communication sessions by managing the cryptographic keys used for authentication and encryption between trackside and on-board equipment.
