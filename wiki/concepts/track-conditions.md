# Track Conditions

**Source:** [[subset40]] | **Date:** 2026-05-07 | **Type:** concept

**Track conditions** are static or temporary track features that affect train operation, such as powerless sections, areas requiring air tightness, or brake tests. ETCS can announce these conditions in advance to the train driver.

## Announcement Distances (§4.2.4)

The minimum announcement distance for track conditions depends on the type:

| Condition | Minimum Announcement Distance |
|---|---|
| Powerless section with pantograph lowering | **17 seconds** at line speed [¶124](http://localhost:8765/go.html#subset40-124) |
| Powerless section without pantograph lowering | **11 seconds** at line speed [¶124](http://localhost:8765/go.html#subset40-124) |
| Other conditions (non-stopping area, air tightness, brake switches) | **10 seconds** at line speed [¶126](http://localhost:8765/go.html#subset40-126) |
