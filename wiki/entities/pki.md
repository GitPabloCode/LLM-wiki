# Public Key Infrastructure (PKI)

**Source:** [[subset26-1]] | **Date:** 2026-05-12 | **Type:** entity

The Public Key Infrastructure (PKI) manages and distributes digital certificates to allow secure on-line distribution of cryptographic keys between KMCs and from a KMC to ERTMS/ETCS entities (on-board equipment, RBCs and RIUs). [subset26-1 ¶125]
