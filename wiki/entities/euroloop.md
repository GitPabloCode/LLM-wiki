# Euroloop

**Source:** [[subset40]] | **Date:** 2026-05-07 | **Type:** entity

**Euroloop** is a trackside transmission system that — like balises — communicates with onboard ETCS equipment. Unlike balises, loops provide continuous transmission over a section of track.

## Installation Rules (§4.1.3)

General installation must respect SUBSET-044 §§6.1.3, 6.10, 6.11, 6.13, 7.8 [¶96](http://localhost:8765/go.html#subset40-96).
