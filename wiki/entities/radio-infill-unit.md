# Radio Infill Unit (RIU)

**Source:** [[subset26]] | **Date:** 2025-07-17 | **Type:** entity

The Radio Infill Unit (RIU) is a trackside device that operates on Level 1 lines, providing signalling information in advance of the next main signal. [subset26 ¶120][subset26 ¶121]

## Function

The RIU transmits infill information to on-board equipment in Level 1 areas via Euroradio, allowing the train to receive updated signalling information before reaching the next main signal. [subset26 ¶1096][subset26 ¶1120]

## Radio Infill Area

A radio infill area begins at a location defined by an order from a balise group and ends at the next main signal balise group. [subset26 ¶1139][subset26 ¶1140]

## Communication

Radio infill sessions use the same protocols as Level 2/3 communication. [subset26 ¶1126]
