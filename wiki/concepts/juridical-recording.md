# Juridical Recording

**Source:** [[subset26]] | **Date:** 2025-07-17 | **Type:** concept

Juridical Recording is the function by which ERTMS/ETCS on-board equipment transmits information to the on-board recording device for legal purposes. [subset26 ¶2818][subset26 ¶2828]

## Data Recorded

The on-board recording device (which is not part of ERTMS/ETCS on-board equipment) receives data from the on-board for:
- Event logging
- Safety-critical event recording
- Post-incident analysis

## Relationship

The on-board provides time-stamped data (UTC for juridical data, local time for driver display). The juridical recording unit is external to the ERTMS/ETCS on-board. [subset26 ¶2811][subset26 ¶2819]
